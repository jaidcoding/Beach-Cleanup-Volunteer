Welcome to the Blue Future Group

Our Mission

Our mission is to protect and preserve the beauty of our coastline for future generations. We believe that clean beaches are essential for the health of our marine ecosystems and the well-being of our communities.

Who We Are…

The Blue Future Club is a non-profit, community education organization dedicated to enhancing eco-friendly waste management on our shores. Our organization was founded in 1999 and is overseen by a volunteer board made up of representatives from businesses, industry, environmental organizations, and civic associations. As an affiliate of the local County, we continually strive to keep our beaches clean, beautiful, and safe for the future of our community.

Why We Do It …

Beach cleanups offer a fantastic opportunity to help protect the marine environment and generate public awareness while getting some exercise! During our two-hour cleanups, we collect trash from a local beach and document our findings, contributing to a long-term study on marine pollution. Each event begins with a brief presentation on local beach debris issues and practical ways to safeguard our oceans. At the end of the cleanup, we weigh the collected trash to track our impact. It’s a fun and educational experience for kids as well!

How It All Started …

The Blue Future Club was founded in 1999 by the local mayor and a beach restaurant owner who wanted to take a stand against marine pollution. In the early years, BFC focused primarily on campaigns to protect individual species, such as birds and sea turtles. Although we have celebrated several victories, it became clear that these species couldn’t be protected without safeguarding their habitats and implementing strong, effective policies. This realization prompted us to think bigger, expand our programs, and adopt a broader, ecosystem-based approach to conservation.

Who Can Sign Up …

The Blue Future Club is committed to fostering an inclusive environment where all volunteers are valued and respected. We welcome participation from individuals of all backgrounds and experiences, without discrimination or harassment of any kind. We encourage volunteers with diverse skills and experiences to join us—perfection is not required! By working together, we can grow the Blue Future Club into a stronger, more inclusive community dedicated to protecting our oceans.
